Live demo of @qrlwallet/connect. Pair a dApp with MyQRLWallet over an end-to-end encrypted channel.

QRL Connect fills WalletConnect's role for the Quantum Resistant Ledger: a self-hosted relay with post-quantum encryption (ML-KEM-768) and native Q-addresses. The dApp gets a standard EIP-1193 provider; the wallet keeps all keys and approvals; the relay sees only ciphertext.

Pick a wallet below (EIP-6963 discovery finds the QRL browser extension and MyQRLWallet automatically), then send a transaction, sign post-quantum messages, and watch the encrypted traffic in the event log.
